Using Our Online Sizing Chart:

  1. Drag the slider button left (-) and right (+) to resize the page objects.
  2. Measure the US quarter, credit card, or ruler lines to confirm the size.
  3. Press the lockpad to lock in your size.
  4. Compare your heel tips against the outline on the screen.
  5. Black dots indicate availability in black, beige dots indicate beige.

Please note that this chart requires a modern browser, and is still an experiment.
