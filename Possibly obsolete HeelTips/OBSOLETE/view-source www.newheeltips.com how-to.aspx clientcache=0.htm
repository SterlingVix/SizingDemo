<h1>how to replace your heel tips</h1>
</div>
<!-- Content -->
<div class="zoneContent">
To replace a pair of heel tips you need a pair of pliers. If you don't have any ask your neighbor. Also, many supermarkets sell basic pliers nowadays.<br />
<br />
If you have a hammer bring it as well. Although that's not as important.<br />
<br />
It's really easy! Just follow the instructions below.<br />
<br />
<table width="100%" cellspacing="0" cellpadding="0" border="0">
<tbody>
<tr>
<td><img width="330" height="248" src="/Files/howto-images/pull_out_old_heeltip.aspx" alt="" /></td>
<td>&nbsp;</td>
<td width="340"><img width="330" height="248" src="/Files/howto-images/old_heeltip_coming_out.aspx" alt="" /></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign="top"><br />
With your pliers try to get a hold of the old heeltip and pull it out.<br />
&nbsp;</td>
<td>&nbsp;</td>
<td valign="top"><br />
Move left and right if it doesn't come out right away. Eventually the tip will come out.<br />
&nbsp;</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><img width="330" height="248" src="/Files/howto-images/insert_new_heeltip.aspx" alt="" /></td>
<td>&nbsp;</td>
<td width="340"><img width="330" height="248" src="/Files/howto-images/drive_in_new_heeltip.aspx" alt="" /></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><br />
Simply replace the old heel tip with the new one and use a hammer to drive it into the heel of your shoe.<br />
&nbsp;</td>
<td width="10">&nbsp;</td>
<td width="340"><br />
If you don't have a hammer you can knock the shoe against a hard surface. Just make sure that you don't damage anything.<br />
&nbsp;</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>
